FAQ

Everything you need to know

Is there really no credit card required?+
None at all. Sign up with your email, use every feature for 14 days, and we only ask for payment details when you decide to stay. No surprise charges, ever.
How is ZenaRank different from Ahrefs or Semrush?+
Ahrefs starts at $129/mo and Semrush at $139/mo — both built for SEO agencies. ZenaRank starts at $29/mo and is designed for founders and small teams who want clear, actionable insights without a 6-week learning curve. We focus on the 20% of features that drive 80% of results.
How does the AI Content Brief work?+
Enter a target keyword. Our AI analyses the top-ranking pages on Google, identifies content gaps, extracts questions people are asking, and produces a structured brief with recommended headings, target word count, competitor insights, and topical clusters — in about 10 seconds. Powered by Claude.
How accurate is the rank tracking?+
We check rankings daily using real Google search results, not cached data. Results are accurate to within 1–2 positions of what you'd see if you searched yourself. We track both desktop and mobile, and support local rank tracking by city.
Can I track multiple websites?+
Yes. Starter supports 1 project, Pro supports 5 projects, and Agency supports unlimited projects with white-label PDF reporting for client work.
Do you offer refunds?+
If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan, email hello@zenarank.com and we'll give you a full refund — no questions asked.
Is there an API?+
Yes — public API access is included in the Agency plan. You can pull keyword data, rank positions, and audit results into your own tools or dashboards. Documentation coming at launch.
