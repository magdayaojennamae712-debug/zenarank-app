    FAQ

    Got questions? We've got answers.

    Is there really no credit card required for the trial?+
    None at all. Sign up with just your email, use every feature for 14 days, and we only ask for payment details when you decide to stay. No surprise charges, ever.
    How is ZenaRank different from Ahrefs or Semrush?+
    Ahrefs starts at $129/mo and Semrush at $139/mo — both designed for SEO agencies with a full team. ZenaRank starts at $29/mo and is designed for founders and small teams who want clear, actionable insights without a months-long learning curve. We focus on the 20% of features that drive 80% of results — and we add AI that guides you on what to fix first.
    What exactly is in each pricing plan?+
    Starter ($29/mo) — 1 project, 100 keywords, 500-page site audit, 5 AI briefs/month, backlink overview, weekly reports. Perfect for one business just getting started.

    Pro ($79/mo) — 5 projects, 500 keywords, unlimited audit pages, 25 AI briefs/month, competitor gap analysis, local SEO, GSC integration, CSV/PDF export. Best for growing businesses.

    Agency ($149/mo) — Unlimited projects and keywords, white-label PDF reports, public API, 5 team seats, GA4 + GSC integration, dedicated account manager, custom onboarding. Built for agencies.
    How does the AI Content Brief work?+
    Enter a target keyword. Our AI (powered by Claude) analyses the top-ranking pages on Google for that keyword, identifies content gaps, extracts questions people are searching for, and produces a structured brief with recommended headings, target word count, competitor insights, and topical clusters — in about 10 seconds. You can export to Notion, Google Docs, or download as PDF.
    How accurate is the rank tracking?+
    We check rankings daily using real Google search results — not cached data. Results are accurate to within 1–2 positions of what you'd see if you searched manually. We track both desktop and mobile, and support local rank tracking by city for businesses with a local audience.
    Do you offer refunds?+
    Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied within your first 30 days, email hello@zenarank.com and we'll issue a full refund. No questions asked.
    Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?+
    Absolutely. Upgrade anytime and get access to the new plan immediately. Downgrade at the end of your billing period — your data is kept safely for 90 days if you downgrade, so you won't lose anything.
