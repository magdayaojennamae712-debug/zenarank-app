Common Questions

FAQ

Is ZenaRank really powered by real AI? ▼
Yes — ZenaRank uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) to power all content generation, analysis, and strategy features. Every blog post, competitor analysis, keyword research result, and recovery plan is generated live by Claude AI in real time.
Does it work for Finnish and Nordic markets? ▼
Absolutely. ZenaRank was built with Nordic markets in mind. The AI Blog Generator supports English, Finnish (Suomi), and Swedish. Keyword research can be filtered by country (Finland, Sweden, Norway). The Local SEO Checker is optimized for local businesses in Helsinki, Tampere, Oulu, Stockholm, and other Nordic cities.
What makes ZenaRank different from Ahrefs or SEMrush? ▼
Ahrefs and SEMrush are built for enterprise SEO teams and cost €100–€500/month. ZenaRank is built for small businesses, local businesses, and small agencies who need real results without a steep learning curve. Our 5 unique features (SERP Preview, E-E-A-T Scorer, Content Gap Radar, Local SEO Checker, Rank Recovery Coach) don't exist in any other single tool.
How long does the free trial last? ▼
14 days, full access, no credit card required. You can use every feature without entering any payment information. If you decide to continue, you'll be asked to choose a plan on day 14.
Can I use ZenaRank for multiple client websites? ▼
Yes. The Pro plan supports 3 websites and the Agency plan supports unlimited websites with a full client management portal, white-label PDF reports with your own branding, and team seats for your colleagues.
Do I need technical SEO knowledge to use ZenaRank? ▼
No. ZenaRank is designed for non-technical users. Every audit finding comes with a plain-English explanation of what it means and what to do. The AI tools do the heavy lifting — you just review, approve, and publish.
