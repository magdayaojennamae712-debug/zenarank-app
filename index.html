FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Is there really no credit card required for the trial?+
Absolutely none. You sign up with just your email, explore every feature for 14 days, and we only ask for payment details when you decide to stay. No surprise charges.
How is ZenaRank different from Ahrefs or Semrush?+
Ahrefs and Semrush are built for SEO agencies managing 50+ clients. They're powerful but overwhelming and expensive ($99–$449/mo). ZenaRank gives you the 20% of features that drive 80% of results, with an AI layer that tells you exactly what to fix — designed for founders and small teams.
Can I track multiple websites?+
Yes. Starter supports 1 project, Pro supports 5 projects, and Agency supports unlimited projects with white-label reporting for client work.
How does the AI content brief work?+
You enter a target keyword. Our AI analyzes the top-ranking pages on Google for that keyword, identifies content gaps, extracts the questions people are asking, and produces a structured brief with recommended headings, target word count, competitor insights, and topical clusters. It takes about 10 seconds and uses Claude under the hood.
How accurate is the rank tracking?+
We check rankings daily using real Google search results (not cached data) via the SerpAPI. Results are accurate to within 1–2 positions of what you'd see searching yourself. We track both desktop and mobile, and support local rank tracking by city.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings — no questions asked, no phone calls required. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer refunds?+
If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan, we'll give you a full refund — no questions asked. Just email support@zenarank.com.
