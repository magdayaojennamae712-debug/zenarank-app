FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is there really no credit card required for the trial?+
None at all. Sign up with just your email, use every feature for 14 days, and we only ask for payment details when you decide to stay. No surprise charges, ever.
How is ZenaRank different from Ahrefs or Semrush?+
Ahrefs starts at $129/mo and Semrush at $139/mo — both designed for SEO agencies with a full team. ZenaRank starts at $29/mo and is built for founders who want clear, actionable insights without a steep learning curve. We focus on the 20% of features that drive 80% of results, and AI guides you on what to fix first.
What exactly is in each plan?+
Starter ($29/mo) — 1 project, 100 keywords, 500-page audit, 5 AI briefs/month, backlink overview. Perfect for one business.

Pro ($79/mo) — 5 projects, 500 keywords, unlimited audit, 25 AI briefs, competitor gap, local SEO, GSC. Best for growing businesses.

Agency ($149/mo) — Unlimited everything, white-label reports, API, 5 team seats, GA4 + GSC, dedicated manager. Built for agencies.
How does the AI Content Brief work?+
Enter a target keyword. Our AI (powered by Claude) analyses the top-ranking pages on Google, identifies content gaps, extracts PAA questions, and produces a structured brief with H2 structure, target word count, competitor insights, and topical clusters — in about 10 seconds. Export to Notion, Google Docs, or PDF.
How accurate is the rank tracking?+
We check rankings daily using real Google search results, not cached data. Results are accurate to within 1–2 positions of what you'd see manually. We track both desktop and mobile, and support local rank tracking by city.
Do you offer refunds?+
Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan, email hello@zenarank.com for a full refund, no questions asked.
